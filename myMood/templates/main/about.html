About Us

my.Mood is a simple micro-blogging web app that focuses on short posts, tweets or notes about what a user wants to say with what they currently feel, or their mood.

This can be a mini-social network app but with no like, comment and user messaging systems and image sharing. It mainly focuses in the posting of stories with words and a follow system between users.
